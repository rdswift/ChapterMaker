Shifting Times

Chapter times can be shifted by entering the number of seconds in the box. Entering a positive number will shift the times forward (later), while entering a negative number will shift the times backwards (earlier).

Note that the shift will be applied to all times in the list. When shifting backwards, any resulting negative times will be set to 00:00:00.00000. This may result in multiple zero time entries.
