When Things Go Wrong

In spite of all the planning, testing and best efforts, occasionally something gets missed and things go wrong. Most error messages generated by the program will give you some idea of the cause of the problem. In those cases where a problem is recurring and cannot be resolved using the information in the error message, please report the problem at https://sourceforge.net/p/chaptermaker/tickets/ including as much information about the problem as possible. We will review the problem and may contact you for additional information to ensure a proper solution.
