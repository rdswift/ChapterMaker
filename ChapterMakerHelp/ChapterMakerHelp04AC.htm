Manual Time Entry / Editing

Chapter times can also be added manually by entering the time in the Chapter Time box and clicking the button.

Individual chapter times can be manually edited or deleted. When an existing time in the list is selected, it will appear in the Chapter Time text box. The time can then be modified by making the desired changes and clicking the button, or deleted from the list by clicking the button.
