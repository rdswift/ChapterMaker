Manual Title Entry / Editing

Chapter titles can also be added manually by entering the title in the Chapter Title box and clicking the button. The title will be inserted above the selected title in the list.

Individual chapter titles can be manually edited or deleted. When an existing title in the list is selected, it will appear in the Chapter Title text box. The title can then be modified by making the desired changes and clicking the button, or simply by editing it in place in the list.

Items can be deleted from the list by selecting them and clicking the button.
